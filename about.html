About LibTechWomen

LibTechWomen is a supportive space for women and their friends to network, develop skills, build confidence, and lead positive change.

Whether you're an experienced or aspiring technologist, whether technology is a hobby or part of your job, you belong here.

We hang out online in the #libtechwomen chatroom on IRC, where we hold a monthly chat. Or, you can find us in Slack; fill out the request form to join. We also organize a meet-up at various conferences such as Code4Lib, ALA, and ACRL.

Find us in multiple social media channels, Google Group listserv, and more below! Our Twitter hashtag is #libtechwomen.
